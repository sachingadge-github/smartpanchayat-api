✦ Features

Everything your village needs,
in one app

From filing a road complaint to downloading a birth certificate — Smart Panchayat handles it all without a single office visit.

📋

File & Track Complaints

Report road damage, broken streetlights, or water supply issues. Attach a photo, auto-tag your location, and track resolution in real time with a reference number.

📄

Digital Certificates

Apply online for Birth, Death, Income and Residence certificates. Download verified PDFs directly to your phone. No queues, no paperwork, no bribes.

💧

Water Bill Payments

View outstanding dues, pay online in seconds, and get an instant digital receipt. All your billing history in one transparent place.

📢

Village Notices

Receive Gram Sabha announcements, government scheme updates, water supply alerts, and panchayat decisions the moment they are published.

🏛️

Government Schemes

Browse 20+ central and state schemes — PM Kisan, PMAY, Ayushman Bharat, MGNREGA and more. Check eligibility and apply with step-by-step guidance.

🤖

AI Help Assistant

Ask questions in Marathi or Hindi and get instant answers about complaint status, required documents, and scheme eligibility — available 24 × 7.

✦ How It Works

Up and running in 3 steps

No paperwork. No passwords. Just your mobile number.

1

Enter your mobile

Login with your Indian mobile number. Verify with a 6-digit OTP. No account creation, no password to remember.

2

Select your village

Pick your Gram Panchayat from the list. The app loads your village's officers, notices, and history automatically.

3

Use all services

File complaints, apply for certificates, pay bills, and read notices — all for free, in your language.

✦ Who It's For

Built for citizens.
Powerful for officers.

Two distinct experiences in one app — every role in the village ecosystem gets what they need.

👨‍🌾

Citizens

Every resident can access panchayat services from anywhere, anytime — completely free.

  • File and track civic complaints
  • Apply & download certificates online
  • Pay water bills, get digital receipts
  • Read village notices & scheme info
  • Get AI-assisted help in your language
🏛️

Panchayat Officers

Officers get a powerful dashboard to manage complaints, issue certificates, and publish notices.

  • Manage and update complaint statuses
  • Process certificate applications

  • View village-level analytics
  • Manage water billing records
