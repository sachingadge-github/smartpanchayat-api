Privacy Policy

Smart Panchayat is committed to protecting your privacy and your data.

📅 Last updated: July 2026 📱 App: Smart Panchayat 📦 com.nexbuild.smartpanchayat

1 Introduction

Smart Panchayat ("we", "our", or "the app") is a digital governance platform developed by NexBuild to connect citizens with their Gram Panchayat for civic services including complaint registration, certificate applications, bill payments, and official notices.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used and protected. By using the app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

🔒 We do not sell your personal data to anyone. Ever.

We do not collect: passwords (OTP-only login), payment card numbers, Aadhaar numbers, or any biometric data.

3 How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate you via OTP and maintain your session securely
  • Register your complaints and route them to the correct panchayat officer
  • Process certificate and document applications
  • Send push notifications about complaint status, new notices, and payment reminders
  • Display relevant government schemes based on your village and profile
  • Improve app stability and fix crashes using anonymous device diagnostics

5 Data Security

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS 1.2+) — encrypted in transit
  • Authentication tokens are stored in device secure storage (expo-secure-store), not plain storage
  • Panchayat admin access is role-based — officers can only see their own panchayat's data
  • No passwords are stored — the app uses OTP-only login
  • Uploaded files are stored on secured cloud servers with access controls

7 Data Retention

Your profile and complaint records are retained as long as your account is active and as required by government record-keeping regulations for gram panchayat operations.

If you delete your account, your personal profile data is removed within 30 days. Complaint and certificate records may be retained by the panchayat as official government records per applicable law.

8 Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data we hold
  • Correction: Update incorrect name, mobile number, or address in your profile
  • Deletion: Request account deletion — we will remove your profile data within 30 days
  • Opt-out: Disable push notifications at any time from your device settings

To exercise any of these rights, email us at the address in Section 11.

9 Children's Privacy

Smart Panchayat is intended for citizens aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has registered, please contact us and we will delete their account immediately.

10 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when features change or regulations require it. We will notify registered users via push notification and update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
